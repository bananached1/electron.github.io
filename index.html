Who are we?

We are a group of year 9s from Hitchin Boys school that are very interested in stem, team work and business. so we entered the F1 in schools UK. We are now professional class which is the second class in the F1 in schools above Entry and Development class.


Our involvement in F1 in Schools UK represents more than just a competition; it's a testament to our collective commitment to learning, growth, and achievement. Through countless hours of research, design iterations, and strategic planning, we've honed our abilities not only in STEM disciplines but also in effective teamwork and project management. Each member of our team brings unique strengths and perspectives to the table, fostering a dynamic environment where collaboration thrives, and ideas flourish. As we navigate the challenges of the Professional Class, we're not just aiming for victory but also for personal and collective development that will shape our future endeavors.
